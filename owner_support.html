Owner Guide

Registering New Buildings

  • Log in to your owner account on the application using your credentials.
  • Navigate to the "Manage Buildings" or "List of Registered Buildings" section in the menu.
  • Click on the "Register Building" button to start registering a new building to the system.
  • Provide a floor plan of the building as a graph, indicating nodes for compartments and edges with values based on factors such as distance between compartments, width of pathways, etc.
  • Assign names or labels to each node (compartment) for easy identification and navigation.
  • Define heuristic values for nodes, estimating the difficulty of reaching the goal node from each node.
  • Save the information to complete the registration process for the new building.

Updating the existing building

  • When updating building details such as node coordinates or heuristic values, you can use '#' as a placeholder in the input fields.
  • Entering '#' in a field indicates that you want to preserve the existing value for that attribute and do not wish to update it.
  • By using '#' strategically, you can selectively update only the attributes you want, leaving others unchanged. For example, if you want to update the coordinate X of a node but keep the existing coordinate Y, enter '#' in the coordinate Y field.
  • Use '#' to clearly communicate your intentions to the system when updating building graph data. It helps in distinguishing between attributes that require updates and those that should remain as they are.

Giving Alerts for Registered Buildings

If there is an emergency situation in one of your registered buildings and you need to alert occupants and authorities:

  • Access the "Emergency Alert" or "Trigger Alert" feature in the application dashboard.
  • Select the building where the emergency is occurring from your registered buildings list.
  • Confirm the alert trigger, and the application will notify registered users, security personnel, and emergency responders about the situation.

Seeking Professional Help

For the most optimal usage of the application and to ensure effective emergency management:

  • Consult with professional emergency response experts to customize and optimize emergency protocols for your buildings.
  • Organize training sessions and drills for building occupants and staff to familiarize them with emergency procedures and the use of the application.
  • Regularly review and update building information, emergency contacts, and alert settings to maintain readiness.
